# mentalhealthcommunity
Nowadays , we see mental health considered as a taboo, people dont consider it as an illness instead keep ignoring it.
As there are lots of communities nowadays include tech communities that are free to take part in .
So we thought to make a mental health community which is free for everyone , anyone of any age group can be a part of it.
It is designed using HTML,CSS,JAVASCRIPT.
It contains a chatbot that will help anyone to talk about their problems if they dont have anyone to talk about.
It has  an anxiety quiz that is we are having plans for future, it will determine one's anxiety level and tell them do they need any medication or not.
It has community sessions, group outings and many more that will help a person in all ways.
We have a quick solution and faqs  option that will help a person to know things easily.
It contains a contact form that will let one join our community
