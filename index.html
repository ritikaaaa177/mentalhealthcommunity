Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes the emotional, psychological, and social well-being of a person. It affects how we think, feel, and act.

Are there cures of mental health problems?

Yes ofcourse there are cure to mental health issues,talking to people , medications , counselling and trips , spending more time with loved ones etc.

How do i know if i am unwell?

if you think your thoughts, feelings and your mind are not in your control and they are slipping away in different direction you need to look after it.

What should i do if i am worried about a friend or relative ?

Talk to them gently dont say them you keep cribbing about your problems what they need is an ear to listen to them. Try being a good listener and dont compare their problems with other people just listen to them.
